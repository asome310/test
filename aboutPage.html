Mission

STEAMGirls is an organization formed out of the Dos Pueblos Women in Technology Club. Founded in November of 2017, it was created for the purpose of encouraging young girls to pursue their passions, and spark interest in STEM-related fields. We are currently teaching Scratch coding and tinkerCAD classes at two local elementary schools.

Meet the Team

Anjali Thakrar

Co-Founder, President

Anjali is a senior at Dos Pueblos High School in Santa Barbara, California. She is passionate about STEM and creating equal opportunities for all students to explore technology. As president of the Women in Technology Club at her school, she was inspired to create opportunities for young girls to explore coding, especially those at economically disadvantaged schools. With STEAMGirls, she hopes to empower young girls to pursue their passions with confidence, vigor, and tenacity, whether these passion lie in STEM or elsewhere.
